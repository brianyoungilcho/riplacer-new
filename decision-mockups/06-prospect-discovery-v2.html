What the UI should show (regardless of option)

We’ll display “facts + evidence” (sources), and keep private user notes separate.

Account dossier sections

  • Contract: size, term, renewal/expiration estimate, incumbent vendor, change history
  • People: supporters/opponents/neutral stakeholders + roles + confidence
  • Signals: budget cycle, leadership change, elections, public sentiment, audits
  • Suggested angles: mapped to your competitive advantages
  • Sources: 3–10 best links (minutes, budgets, RFPs, news) with pull quotes

Rep augmentation

  • Private notes: “inside baseball” (not public)
  • Deal posture: champion identified? landmine? procurement timeline?
  • Next steps: meeting targets, outreach sequence, objections
  • Generated outputs: talk track + email draft + call opener

My recommendation

Start with Option B. It’s the cleanest mapping to your workflow, keeps reps focused, and it’s implementable by evolving the existing `DiscoveryTab` + adding a right-side drawer.
